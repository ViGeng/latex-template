\subsection{Collaborating with an AI reviewer}

Use \texttt{\textbackslash ai} to hand a span of text to an AI together with an instruction: quote what you want acted on and say what to do. By default the AI should apply it now: \ai{The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}{rewrite in a formal academic tone} Mark a request as \texttt{[hold]} to park it for later instead of acting: \ai[hold]{Our method is 10x faster.}{verify against the results table before keeping}
